Eurosonic Livestream

On Friday January 15th we'll be playing Grunnsonic. The event aired as a livestream on January 15th 2021. You can watch it below.

The line-up has been put together by POPgroningen, the pop-organization of the Province of Groningen, in collaboration with ESNS. Local acts present themselves to the public and music professionals during ESNS in an entirely digital program. The program consists of showcases and interviews with the selected acts.
